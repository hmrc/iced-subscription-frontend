Access

Advanced Tariff Rulings (ATaR)

From this page you can enrol to the Advanced Tariff Rulings (ATaR) service

You can use this service to obtain a written tariff classification of your goods. It's not a legal requirement, but provides assurance that your goods have the correct commodity code.

Before you move goods in or out of the UK, or carry out other customs activities, you may need a GB Economic Operator and Registration Identification (EORI) number. HMRC use this to identify you and collect duty on your goods.

Don't have a GB EORI yet? Register for an EORI

You will also need the Government Gateway user ID and password that you use:

  • for your business or organisation
  • for yourself, if you are applying as an individual

You might need to tell us

If you haven't registered for a customs service before you may need:

  • A GB EORI number
  • National Insurance number (if you are an individual or sole trader)
  • your Unique Taxpayer Reference (UTR)

You can find the following information on the Companies House register

  • the date you started your business

It takes around 5 to 10 minutes to apply to access the service. You should get it immediately
